About MISELFS

Inspired by the biggest trend on the web, MISELFS is an art-based NFT collection with 200+ initial traits meant to capture and express the originality of those who engage with this project. The Combination of different colors brings out lots of different personalities and creates a way for people to reflect their uniqueness and creativity by producing a new path that allows the flow of our passion for art, style, and fashion into the digital.
